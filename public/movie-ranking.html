The Best Movies According To Deepak

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

Three friends. One genius. A system questioned. Rancho, the free spirit, teaches us to chase excellence — not marks. In a world ruled by pressure and rote, It screams "All is well" — if your heart truly floats.

Chichoree

A nostalgic dive into college days, where friendship, failure, and fun collide. A father’s heartbreak meets a story of losers who never gave up. Laughter, love, and lessons — that success isn’t everything, But the fight, the fall, and the rise... that’s what makes life worth living.

Bahubali

A tale of kingdoms, betrayal, and might, Of Mahendra and Amarendra — father and son shining bright. With waterfalls, wars, and whispers of fate, “Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?” — kept us awake.
